ABOUT US

Established in the illustrious year of 1990 by visionary group of Jadia brothers , Kamlesh Jadia, Brajesh Jadia, and Suresh Jadia, our diamond company has been a beacon of elegance, crafting timeless pieces that narrate stories of love and sophistication. With over three decades of expertise, we have perfected the art of transforming raw brilliance into exquisite gems. Nestled in the heart of Mumbai at JE-4160, Tower J East Core, Bharat Diamond Bourse, Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra East, Maharashtra 400051 . Here, tradition meets innovation, as each diamond undergoes meticulous craftsmanship, bearing the hallmark of our unparalleled artistry.
